palestinian/canadian in newyork/montréal · by rasha lama 2023

○ Here is a circle as:

the tattoo on the hip of the woman;
the fertile entry of the Araf Triangle,
the decided birthplace of Homo sapiens,
the soil that materialized the primordial;
a burning core stamped as a conscious circle,
the bolt attaching the material to the eternal
through the fabric of the human body.
